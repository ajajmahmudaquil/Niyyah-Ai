----------------------------------------------------
- On signup and reset-password pages, show a live password strength indicator:
  - Labels: Weak / Medium / Strong
  - Animated progress bar that updates as user types (smooth transitions)
  - Show checklist of requirements (uppercase, lowercase, number, special, length)
- Strength logic:
  - Weak: fewer than 3 requirements met OR length < 8
  - Medium: length >= 8 AND 3–4 requirements met
  - Strong: length >= 10 AND all requirements met
- Add subtle animation (CSS transition) for bar fill and label change.

Login:

  - Call `supabase.auth.resetPasswordForEmail(email, { redirectTo: APP_URL + "/reset-password" })`
